Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know About Our Services

Get answers to the most common questions about Ever Centaury's healthcare, catering, and care management services.

What makes Ever Centaury one of the best healthcare providers?

Ever Centaury employs over 5,000 healthcare professionals delivering excellence across our three core services. With €150+ million annual turnover and 7.5 million community healthcare interventions since 2016, we've established unmatched operational excellence and scale in healthcare education, long-term care, and contract catering.

How does your DBOM model work for long-term care facilities?

Our Design, Build, Operate, and Maintain model provides end-to-end responsibility for healthcare facilities. We handle everything from resident-centered design and construction to daily operations and maintenance, ensuring cost predictability, risk mitigation, and high operational standards throughout the project lifecycle.

What certifications and quality standards do you maintain?

We maintain comprehensive certifications including ISO 22000:2018 (Food Safety), ISO 9001:2015 (Quality Management), ISO 14001:2015 (Environmental Management), HACCP compliance, Halal certification, and SA 8000:2014 (Social Accountability). Our Dubai training center is KHDA certified.

How many meals do you produce daily and for which facilities?

Our Food Factory produces over 9,000 meals daily, serving many public hospitals, 2,800+ residents across long-term care facilities, and providing in-flight catering for 35+ international airlines. Our 21,000 sqm facility has capacity for 50,000+ meals daily.

What training and education programs do you offer?

We operate international training centers offering Pearson BTEC International diplomas in Health and Social Care, IELTS certification, and specialized healthcare training programs. Our centers feature hospital simulation rooms, nutrition labs, and partnerships with leading educational institutions worldwide.

How extensive is your domiciliary care service coverage?

We provide comprehensive domiciliary care services to 7,700+ clients, delivering over 100,000 interventions monthly. Our services include medical care, personal assistance, home help, specialized dementia care, and palliative care, supporting independent living in the community.

How do you ensure continuity of care during emergencies?

Ever Centaury maintains robust emergency preparedness protocols and contingency planning across all our facilities. Our experienced teams are trained for rapid deployment and crisis management, ensuring uninterrupted healthcare services and patient safety under any circumstances.

How do you ensure food safety and quality in your catering operations?

Our Grade A certified Food Factory operates under stringent HACCP monitoring with 150 licensed food handlers. We utilize cook-chill technology, remote monitoring systems, and maintain ISO 22000:2018 certification. Our facility includes dedicated halal kitchens and comprehensive quality control measures.

Leading healthcare and service provider delivering excellence across healthcare education, long-term care facilities, and contract catering with proven operational expertise and international quality standards.
